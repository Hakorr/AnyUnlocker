Installation Instructions

Note: If you've had fish fly to your face before (or tried other unlocking methods), delete the game & %appdata%/Anymaker folder before anything else!
  1. Press WIN KEY + R on your keyboard to open the Run dialog.
  2. Type %appdata%/Anymaker and hit Enter.
  3. Open or create /saves folder.
  4. Extract/unzip the downloaded creativeLootSave_unzip_me.zip to the /saves folder.
  5. You should see a creativeLootSave folder in the Anymaker/saves/ directory.
  6. Launch the game, load the creativeLootSave world, and go pick up your items!
Note: by default, items matching "Tier -1, Decorative, Building, Furniture or Clothing" are not included! You need to remove the exclusions to add them. Tier -1 is most likely detected, so avoid unlocking items that are tier -1. Such items cannot be unlocked legitimately and may result in your testing access being revoked. You've been warned!

Installation Instructions

Note: If you've had fish fly to your face before (or tried other unlocking methods), delete the game & %appdata%/Anymaker folder before anything else!
  1. Press WIN KEY + R on your keyboard to open the Run dialog.
  2. Type %appdata%/Anymaker and hit Enter.
  3. Replace the unlocks.txt file with the one you downloaded.
  4. Make sure the file is named exactly unlocks.txt and is located in %appdata%/Anymaker/.
  5. Open the game, and enjoy your new items!
Note: by default, items matching "Tier -1, Decorative, Building, Furniture or Clothing" are not included! You need to remove the exclusions to add them. Tier -1 is most likely detected, so avoid unlocking items that are tier -1. Such items cannot be unlocked legitimately and may result in your testing access being revoked. You've been warned! You CANNOT share the unlocks.txt with other people! It's tied to your Steam ID.
