Installation Instructions

  1. Note: If you've had fish fly to your face before (or tried other unlocking methods), delete the game, AND %appdata%/Anymaker folder before anything else!
  2. Press WIN KEY + R on your keyboard to open the Run dialog.
  3. Type %appdata%/Anymaker and hit Enter.
  4. Open or create /saves folder.
  5. Extract/unzip the downloaded creativeLootSave_unzip_me.zip to the /saves folder.
  6. You should see a creativeLootSave folder in the Anymaker/saves/ directory.
  7. Launch the game, load the creativeLootSave world, and go pick up your items!
  8. Note: by default, items matching "Tier -1, Decorative, Building, Furniture or Clothing" are not included in the save! Tier -1 might be detected, so avoid unlocking such items.
