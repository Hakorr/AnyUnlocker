Installation Instructions

Note: If you've had fish fly to your face before (or tried other unlocking methods), empty the %appdata%/Anymaker/unlocks.txt file before anything else!
  1. Close your game. Very important!
  2. WIN KEY + R → type %appdata%\Anymaker → press Enter.
  3. Open unlocks.txt (Notepad or double-click).
  4. Replace the contents with the encrypted text shown in the box.
  5. Save (Ctrl + S) and close the file.
  6. Launch the game and enjoy your new items.
  7. You can't really share the encrypted unlocks.txt, it is tied to your Steam ID.
Note: by default, items matching "Decorative, Building, Furniture, Clothing" are not included! You need to remove the exclusions to add them.

Installation Instructions

Note: If you've had fish fly to your face before (or tried other unlocking methods), empty the %appdata%/Anymaker/unlocks.txt file before anything else!
  1. Press WIN KEY + R on your keyboard to open the Run dialog.
  2. Type %appdata%/Anymaker and hit Enter.
  3. Open or create /saves folder.
  4. Extract/unzip the downloaded creativeLootSave_unzip_me.zip to the /saves folder.
  5. You should see a creativeLootSave folder in the Anymaker/saves/ directory.
  6. Launch the game, load the creativeLootSave world, and go pick up your items!
Note: by default, items matching "Decorative,Building,Furniture,Clothing" are not included! You need to remove the exclusions to add them.
